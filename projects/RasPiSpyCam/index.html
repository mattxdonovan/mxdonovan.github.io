Tutorial for Push Button Raspberry Pi Spy Cam

What You'll Need

  • 1 Raspberry Pi 3 - Model B
  • 1 Raspberry Camera - V 2.1
  • 1 microSD Card - 16gb is prefered
  • 1 Breadboard
  • 1 Tactile Switch Button
  • 1 Battery Holder with batteries(for making it mobile)
  • 1 Mini HDMI charger
  • Soldering Kit
  • Wires
